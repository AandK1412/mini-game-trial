North Korea Defectors Simulation

Case Background

This simulation takes you through the harrowing journey of North Korean defectors as they navigate the dangers of escape across the Yalu River, hide in China, cross the Gobi Desert, and seek freedom in Mongolia. Players will face real-life challenges, make critical decisions, and experience the risks and resilience behind every step.
